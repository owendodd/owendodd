Background

Artsy's editorial content has undergone many transformations. What started out as a user generated content platform eventually turned into a subscription based content production platform, before transitioning into it's current incarnation as a truly object media arm of the larger company. These many iterations led to a large variety of users engaging with our internal publishing tools to produce a wide range of content with very different purposes.
