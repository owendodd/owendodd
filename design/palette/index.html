The Artsy City Guide was created to enable our user-base to easily discover and visit art in the physical world. The digital experience allows users to follow artists, galleries and museums for updates on new shows, and to navigate to these shows in our 6 launch cities. The product was built as part of an on-going partnership with BMW and was integrated into our existing iOS app.

Background

The Artsy iOS app has existed since 2015 as a tool for art enthusiasts to browse and save artworks, follow artists, browse fairs, and explore gallery and musuem shows. The app experience was ultimately aimed at enabling collectors to purchase works of art via conversations with galleries and by bidding in auctions.
