Information

I'm a product designer and photographer working on projects focused around art, technology, food and publishing. Currently I'm working as a Senior Product Designer for NYT Cooking and based in Copenhagen.

Previously I've spent time developing design systems within The New York Times, designing the editorial experience at Artsy, and have done digital and print based projects at a number of design studios including Berger & Föhr and Zut Alors!.

Additionally, I've founded a food and wine project called Jzuj Food, which ran from 2018–2020 as series of collaborative dinner events, and have published ecologically focused art books as a part of Solar Power Press.
