Other Projects

I'm a designer and photographer working on projects focused around art, technology, food and publishing. Currently I'm working as a Senior Product Designer for NYT Cooking and based in Copenhagen.

Previously I've spent time developing design systems within The New York Times, designing the editorial experience at Artsy, and have done digital and print based projects at a number of design studios including Berger & Föhr and Zut Alors!.

Additionally, I make fruit preserves with Kasia Sznajder at O.K. Jams. From 2018–2020 I ran a food and wine project called Jzuj Food with Nicholas Sewitz. And I have also published ecologically focused art books as a part of Solar Power Press, along with Josef Bull and Amy Ontiveros.
